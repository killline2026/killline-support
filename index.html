Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add a transaction? Tap the + button on the Home tab and fill in the details.
  • How do I edit or delete a transaction? Go to History, tap a transaction, then edit or delete.
  • Why is my balance different? Check that your initial balance and transactions are correct.
  • How do I manage categories? Open Settings > Categories to add, edit, or delete.
  • How does the Kill.Line alert work? It triggers when your balance drops below the threshold you set.

Troubleshooting

  • Force close and reopen the app.
  • Ensure you are on the latest version of iOS.
  • Check your internet connection if you use iCloud sync.
  • If the issue persists, email support with details.
